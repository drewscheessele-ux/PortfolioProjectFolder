How I Work

Research-led, prototype-heavy, and grounded in real user behavior.

01

Investigate the Real Problem

I start with research synthesis: interviews, secondary research, and competitor analysis to find root friction instead of surface symptoms.

02

Structure Insights Into Direction

I convert findings into personas, affinity maps, storyboards, and user flows so each concept is tied to clear user and system needs.

03

Prototype and Stress-Test

I prototype early and often, from paper and Wizard-of-Oz tests to interactive Figma/GPT prototypes, to validate behavior before implementation.

04

Refine for Adoption

I iterate based on qualitative feedback, usability outcomes, and constraints to deliver practical recommendations teams can actually ship.
